# Professional Industrial Website Upgrade

## Overview
The website has been transformed into a **high-stakes, professional industrial-grade** platform with premium design elements and comprehensive image placeholders for NanoBanana AI image generation.

### 1. **Color Scheme & Aesthetics**
- **Dark Industrial Theme**: Deep grays (#0f172a, #1e293b) and black backgrounds
- **Accent Colors**: Professional blue (#3b82f6) for CTAs and highlights
- **High Contrast**: Dramatic lighting effects and shadows for depth
